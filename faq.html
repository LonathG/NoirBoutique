Frequently asked questions
What is a perfume decant?

A decant is a smaller bottle filled with perfume from an original, larger bottle. It's a great way to try luxury scents at an affordable price before committing to a full bottle.

Are your perfumes authentic?

Yes, we only deal with 100% authentic designer and niche perfumes. Every decant is carefully hand-poured from the original bottle upon purchase.

What sizes do you offer?

We currently offer 10ml decants. This size is ideal for travel or for testing a signature scent over several weeks.

How should I store my decants?

To preserve the scent's integrity, keep your decants in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and extreme heat.

How long does delivery take?

For orders within Sri Lanka, delivery usually takes 2-4 business days via our courier partners.

Do you offer returns?

Due to the nature of perfume decants and for hygiene reasons, we do not accept returns. However, if your order arrives damaged, please contact us immediately for a resolution!
